======================
Contributing to MOTECH
======================

Thank you for your interest in contributing to MOTECH. There are many different ways to get involved - regardless of your area of expertise and time commitment, there is likely a useful way for you to contribute. Please peruse the sections below for some instructions on how to get started contributing in specific areas. If there is another way you'd like to help that isn't listed, just `let us know <mailto:motech-dev@googlegroups.com>`_ what your interests are and we can help find a project for you.
